Welcome

Pioneers in Engineering's Final Competition is the epic conclusion of our semester-long robotics competition season. This year, we've had over 300 high school students from 25 different high schools competing to see who can build the best robot. Final competition is where they finally get to pit their robots against each other to see who will take home the gold in this year's game, PIErate Prospectors.

About Pioneers in Engineering

Pioneers in Engineering (PiE) promotes science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM) education through an exciting, mentorship-based process. PiE was founded in 2008 to address the lack of representation of East Bay high schools in the UC Berkeley engineering program. Our organization hopes to provide a sustainable, fun, and hands-on way for students to pursue an interest in science and technology.
To learn more about us, visit our website at pioneers.berkeley.edu.
